How to Carry out the Simplex Algorithm

The Simplex Algorithm is a method of solving linear programming problems. In plain English, it's used to reach a goal while also having constraints. Since this is math, we're dealing with numbers and the formulas just use add, subtract and multiply (which is why it is called linear programming). Typical uses of the simplex algorithm are to find the right mix of ingredients at the lowest cost (the goal). If the ingredients are food, the constraints would be having at least so many calories, so much protein, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, minerals, etc. Of course the simplex algorithm is applicable to much more than food.
